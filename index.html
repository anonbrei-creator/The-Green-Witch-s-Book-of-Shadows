Chapter I
Intentions, Entries & Remedies

As the Charmed Book named its demons, so too does this grimoire name what we face — not to fear it, but to meet it with intention. Each entry names the affliction and prescribes the intentional action that will move, clear, or transform it.

Chapter II
Sacred Chores & Chakra Healing

The home is your temple. Every act of tending it is an act of tending yourself. What we do with our hands, we do with our energy. The most powerful healing often lives in the most ordinary moments — the sweep of a broom, the scrub of a surface, the folding of cloth. Each chore below has an energetic counterpart. Do them with intention, and they become ritual.

Chapter III
Simmer Pots & Kitchen Cauldron

The simmer pot is one of the oldest forms of hearth magic. Fill your pot with intention. Each ingredient is chosen, not just for its fragrance, but for its energetic resonance. Stir clockwise to draw in; counterclockwise to release. Let the steam carry your wishes through every room. The four elements are always present: water holds the spell, earth is in the botanicals, fire is the heat below, and air carries it all.

Base Instructions: Fill pot ¾ with water. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer. Add ingredients with intention. Stir clockwise to attract, counterclockwise to release. Keep water topped off. Discard ingredients in compost when done.

Chapter IV
The Herb Glossary

Every plant has a story — one rooted in the soil but also in centuries of symbolism, folk practice, and energetic correspondence. Learn these allies. Work with one or two at a time. Build a relationship before expanding your apothecary.

Chapter V
Moon Phases & Timing

The moon governs tides, cycles, and the deep interior rhythms of the body. As witches, we align our working with her phases — not because we must, but because doing so places us in harmony with natural forces that have been honored for millennia. Each phase below is a doorway. Click to learn what lives behind it.

Moon Water

Moon water is one of the simplest and most potent tools in the green witch's practice. It is water charged with the light and energy of the moon — and by your intention. Use it anywhere you would use water, and the spell is already cast.

Chapter VI
Crystal & Stone Directory

Crystals are gifts from deep within the earth — each one a record of time, pressure, and transformation. They hold, amplify, and direct energy. You don't need many. Start with one that calls to you. Learn it. Work with it. Let your collection grow the way trust grows — slowly, and with attention.

Chapter VII
The Chakra System

The word chakra comes from Sanskrit, meaning wheel or disk. The chakras are seven spinning energy centers running along the spine from the base to the crown of the head. They are the bridge between the physical body and the energetic body — each one governing a different dimension of your life. When they flow freely, you flow freely. When one is blocked, you feel it — in your body, your emotions, your relationships, and your spirit. Click any chakra to open its full guide.
